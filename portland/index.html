12 — Methodology

How I Built This

Data pipeline: Crash records queried from ODOT’s OTSDE ArcGIS REST API (layer 0), paginated at 10,000 records per call. 14,025 records for 2023 across Multnomah, Washington, and Clackamas counties. Coordinates are native WGS84 (LAT_DD / LONGTD_DD fields).
City assignment: ODOT provides a CITY_SECT_NM field. Portland sections (NE, NW, SE, SW, N, S, Burnside, Bridges) are merged into a single “Portland” entry. Crashes in unincorporated areas are excluded.
Behavioral flags: ALCHL_INVLV_FLG, DRUG_INVLV_FLG, MJ_INVLV_FLG, CRASH_SPEED_INVLV_FLG, and CRASH_HIT_RUN_FLG are pre-computed by ODOT. Injury severity uses KABCO (K=Fatal, A=Serious, B/C=Minor, O=PDO). Hours are ranges (e.g. “03:00 PM to 03:59 PM”), not exact times.

Data: ODOT · Vision Zero Portland · TransGIS
